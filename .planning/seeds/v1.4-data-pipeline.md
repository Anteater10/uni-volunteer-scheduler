# v1.4 Seed — Data Pipeline (PowerBI + Tableau)

**Status:** seed (not yet promoted to ROADMAP). Draft 2026-04-17.
**Goal:** convert the v1.2/v1.3 operational database into a decision-grade
data product that SciTrek staff + UCSB partners can act on weekly.

## North-star questions

1. **Fill rate by (school, quarter, module)** — are we filling slots?
2. **Volunteer funnel** — of people who sign up, how many confirm → attend?
3. **No-show prediction** — which confirmed signups are likely to no-show so
   the organizer can over-book or send a nudge?
4. **Partner scorecard** — per high school: sessions delivered, hours
   contributed, orientation compliance, no-show %.

## Three-layer architecture

### DE — Data Engineering (batch, nightly)

- Celery beat job exports OLTP tables to `exports/warehouse/YYYY-MM-DD/*.parquet`.
- `dbt-postgres` project in `analytics/` builds a **star schema** in a
  separate `warehouse` schema of the same Postgres 16 instance:
  - Dims: `dim_user`, `dim_event`, `dim_module`, `dim_school`, `dim_date`.
  - Facts: `fct_signup`, `fct_slot`, `fct_orientation_credit`,
    `fct_broadcast`.
- Snapshots: `snap_signup_status` (SCD-2 on `signup.status`) so the funnel
  is reconstructable at any historical point.

### DA — Data Analytics (PowerBI)

- Connects to `warehouse.*` via DirectQuery (read-only role `bi_reader`).
- Dashboards:
  - **Ops overview** — fill rate, confirmed-vs-waitlist, upcoming capacity.
  - **Funnel** — signup → confirmed → checked-in, sliced by school + module.
  - **Orientation compliance** — % of confirmed volunteers with a completed
    orientation credit for the module family.

### DS — Data Science (Tableau + notebook)

- **Partner scorecard** in Tableau (published to SciTrek leadership):
  one tile per partner high school with red/amber/green on each KPI.
- **No-show model** (scikit-learn, notebook first, later a Celery task):
  - Features: days since signup, prior no-show count, orientation status,
    time-of-day, weekday, module type, school.
  - Output: `fct_signup.noshow_probability` column refreshed nightly.
  - Consumed by the organizer roster UI as a subtle yellow dot.
